Sony’s Sustainability Vision

Inspire a world filled with emotion
for this generation and beyond

Sony is moving forward with the challenge of value creation driven by creativity, technology, and diversity.
Through our efforts for people, society, and the earth, we aim to inspire a world filled with emotion for this generation and beyond.

PEOPLE

Celebrate individual differences and enrich hearts and minds

SOCIETY

Foster a society in which everyone can live with peace of mind

EARTH

Protect and preserve the Earth, which is the foundation of life
